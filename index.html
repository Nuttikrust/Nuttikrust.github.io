Who Am I?

Hi my name is Miguel Cunha!

I am 19 Years young and currently reside in Johannesburg South, South Africa.

Aspiring web developer who thoroughly enjoys exercising the mind with both creativity and problem solving

I like to read, write with the dream of becoming a full stack web developer.

As of June 10 2019, i have also become an aspiring writer with a book titled "The Man Who Reached 20" in the works, updates will be posted in the Update section of the home page.




Why I Want To Be A Programmer

Once i discovered the excitement and power behind programming i fell in love with the intellectual side of creating my own projects and putting ideas into working formats. The process of learning is hard and daunting but the reward from creating simple objects such as color schemes, setting up a sort of finished has really inspired me to learn more and dive deeper into what i can do with these tools at my disposal. I want to create some top quality apps and websites that will make not only myself proud, but the client as well. This obviously meaning constant improvement to this site as i learn.

My Progress

I have been spending quite a bit of time learning various programming languages such as C# for both server side projects and also game development in Unity. It has been quite a struggle to pick a route to focus on as the mountain of information and lack of a course has made it quite daunting to dive into. So Currently am learning Javascript, HTML, CSS, .NET and C# to broaden my field of knowledge but also give me a further understanding of how tools and programming languages connect between each other and also what they are best used for.
